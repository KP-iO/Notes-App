Help

1)Upon registering to use the app and logging in you will see a blank screen this shows you that there are no notes and now it is time for you to add yours by pressing the add button on the bottom right of the screen.

2)Upon pressing the add button you will be directed to the notes page where you can enter your note

3)By pressing the back button positioned in the top left of the screen your note will be saved and you will be redirected back to the home page where you can view your notes.

4)Once you re enter a note you have the choice to either delete a note by selecting the rubbish bin icon or sharing the contents of the note by selecting the share button

5)If you are ever in the situation where you ahve too many noyes and would like to delete all of them at once you can do this by selecting the menu in the top right here you will see an option "Delete all notes" once pressed all notes will be deleted.
